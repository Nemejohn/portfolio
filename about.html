Overview & Journey

Hi 👋, I'm Nwagboo John based in Lagos Nigeria with Bachelor of Science (BS) in Computer Science I've worked for Google, Meta, Microsoft and pause. What if all of this were deceits. I think Companies are easily carried away with the idea of hiring people with degrees and what company they've worked for, but I believe that skills and passion are what truly matter in the tech industry. I am a developer who is not after perfection but with a strong passion for coding and problem-solving. I have experience in various programming languages and framework, and I am always eager to learn new technologies.

Back in secondary school, I was privileged to be the first student to ever get a prefectship role as a Time Keeper 1 at senior secondary school one (SS1). While growing up, I had no laptop so I was allegic to anything that had to do with computers and technology because of fear of being laughed at by my mates. I was also not able to use a smartphone until 2022, so I had no idea what programming was all about. But in 2023 after Graduating from secondary school, I had my First laptop and embarked on my journey as a frontend developer and then ventured into the server side (Backend Development). Along the line, I added UIUX design as a skill. With just a year of experience, I successfully established and led a tech company from scratch, demonstrating strong leadership 2 and strategic vision. I am now a Full Stack Developer with a strong focus on building scalable and efficient web applications.
